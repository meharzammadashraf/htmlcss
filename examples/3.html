Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite food. Here is a recipe of delicious scrambled egg.

Ingredients

  • 2egg
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a mediu heat.
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter had melted mix cream and egg.
  4. Using a spatula fold the egg from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you can making an omelette).
  5. When the egg are well moist remove from the heat (it will continue cook on the plate until served).
